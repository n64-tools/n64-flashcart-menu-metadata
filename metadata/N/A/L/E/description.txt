It's a Bumpin', Bruisin', Brawlin' Bash! The many worlds of Nintendo collide in the ultimate showdown of strength and skill! Up to 4 players can choose their favorite characters - complete which their signature attacks - and go at it in Team Battles and Free-For-Alls. Or venture out in your own to conquer the 14 stages in a single-player mode. Either way, Super Smash Bros. is a no-holds barred action-fest that will keep you coming back for more!
