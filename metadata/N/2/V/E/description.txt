Get in a Twist. The Chemeleon is back. Once again our courageous Chemeleon is led into a splendid adventure by the wiley White Rabbit. Using his tongue and his new gadget, a parachute, he can make his way through all the magic worlds, collecting items, changing his clothes (being a chemeleon), beating his enemies as well as locating the many secret romms where there are mini games to enthrall and delight.
