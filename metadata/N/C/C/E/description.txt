The fate of the world lies in your hands. As commander of the United Nations GDI forces, your mission is to stop the reign of terror caused by the neo-fascist terrorist group, the Brotherhood of Nod. Or perhaps you prefer to cause havoc rather than prevent it? Then send your Nod troops into battle against the GDI as you vie for world domination. Either way, you must carefully orchestrate every aspect of your military strategy - from planning attacks to resource management - in order to achieve ultimate victory.
