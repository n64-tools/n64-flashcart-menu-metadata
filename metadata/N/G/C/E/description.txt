The streets are clear, the lights are green and nothing is stopping you from taking the GT 64 Challenge. GT Racing puts the dream of professional racing into the hands of the ordinary driver. Specially re-engineered passenger cars take on the streets from Tokyo to the USA in the search for greater realism and improved challenges. GT 64 takes this one stage further by placing you behind the wheel of one of these monster machines. Accurately modelled car systems demand your every response be as real as if you were actually there, putting your faith in yourself and in the equipment around you.
