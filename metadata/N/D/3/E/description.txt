Drac's back and this time he wants to stay! Take control of Reinhardt Schneider, a whip toting Vampire killer who has waited his whole life to meet his ultimate destiny, or Carrie Fernandez, a young girl with a strong magical presence who is mysteriously drawn to Dracula's evil Castlevania. It is their duty to seek out Castlevania and put an end to its residents plans. What is your destiny?
