It's the ultimate Tom and Jerry showdown! One on one fighting! Tag team tournaments! Just choose your character and then select the location! All environments are modeled after scenes from your favorite Tom and Jerry episodes. There's "The Mice Before Christmas," "Catchin' in the Kitchen" and many more. An "All-Star" lineup of characters such as Tom, Jerry, Butch, Spike, Tuffy, and Duckling help to make this the ultimate Tom and Jerry experience. Weapons include shovels, bombs, red-hot pokers, tennis rackets, furniture and an assortment of fruit (just to mention a few), plus there are countless other tricks, traps and surprises to be found. It doesn't get any better than this. So go to your corner, wait for the bell and come out fighting. Just keep an eye out behind you, because no one fights fair in this game.
