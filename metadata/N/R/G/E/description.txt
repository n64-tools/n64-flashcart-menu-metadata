Scavenge through three games in one! In Reptar Rally, the babies visit Reptar on his candy island to help him collect as many sweets as possible. In Angelica's Temple of Gloom, the babies are transported to the ancient Aztec Temple of Kokobob - find all the pieces to the broken Aztec statue before Angelica does, or else... In Pirate Treasure Hunt, play diaper-to-diaper and search through a sunken pirate ship looking for hidden treasure.
