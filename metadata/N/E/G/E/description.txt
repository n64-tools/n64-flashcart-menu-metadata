TWIST, TURN, AND BURN Pit yourself against 12 laser blasting speedfreaks across 12 looping, coiling, corkscrewing rollercoaster racetracks. If you've never seen a cyber-cycle take a stinger missile up the tailpipe during a blistering halfpipe you're about to get a rush of adrenaline. Racing to the N'th degree through gravitational extremes. Surfing the knife-edge between G-force and burning wreckage! If gravity's a law, then this is a felony.
