A pair of defective nuclear missiles, en route to a safedetonation site has begun to leak. Badly damaged, the carrier automatically locks onto the most direct route. Clear a path to help the carrier to arrive safely. Tons of vehicles are at your disposal. Leave nothing standing or the adventure will end in an earth-shattering explosion! Find the hidden technicians to ensure a safe detonation. Strap on your seat belt, it's going to be a bumpy ride.
