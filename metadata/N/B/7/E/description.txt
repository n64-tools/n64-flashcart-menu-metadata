Grunty returns and that is bad news for Banjo and Kazooie! In this all new adventure, combine everything you learned in the award-winning prequel, Banjo-Kazooie, with dozens of brand-new moves and abilities. Explore eight original worlds like a monstrous factory and a dilapidated amusement park. Solve incredible puzzles that link those worlds together, sometimes you'll have to complete tasks in several worlds to solve a single puzzle!
